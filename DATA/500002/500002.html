About

ABB India Limited is an integrated power equipment manufacturer supplying the complete range of engineering, products, solutions and services in areas of Automation and Power technology. [1]

Key Points

Business Segments
Electrification(38%): This segment provides technology across the full electrical value chain from substation to the point of consumption, enabling safer and more reliable power. A range of digital and connected innovations for low- and medium-voltage, including EV infrastructure, sensing and control, etc. [1]
Motion(38%): This segment provides products, solutions and related services that increase industrial productivity and energy efficiency. Its motors, generators and drives provide power, motion and control for a wide range of automation applications. [2]
Industrial Automation(21%): This segment provides products, systems and services designed to optimize the productivity of industrial processes. Solutions include turnkey engineering, control systems, measurement products, life cycle services, outsourced maintenance and industry specific products. [3]
Robotics and Discrete Automation(3%): This segment provides value-added solutions in robotics, machine and factory automation. [4][5]
